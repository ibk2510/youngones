Young Ones

The term “Young ones” distinctively describes that we are a team to act as a bridge between passionate students and budding young professionals. We aim to connect the most engrossing skills among students through captivating speeches that serve as the ladder for the growth of students.

Vision

To be as a pensive platform that helps students to knock down every possible hindrance in the way of evolving process. To make an impact on the young minds by sharing experiences of young leaders through their veracious talks and attain mutual benefits.

Mission

Our vision is to connect, cultivate and develop the young minds to let them identify their goals and certainly get succeed in it.

The Purpose of Young One!

The main purpose of Young ones is to upgrade possessed skills and enhance individuality with exchange of views. We also provide opportunities to interact with talented and successful leaders. We work on connecting students to leaders primarily based on their interests. We are here to help people who are lost in their path of dream and take efforts to broaden their vision to have clarity in their goals. We act as the much needed motivation and inspiration that many people seek to realise uniqueness.

Benefits of viewers

Through our platform, the viewers get the chance to gain knowledge and chisel their skills that needs to be upgraded.This might be the place where you can find your true inspiration by their speech and follow them to lead a meaningful life but assuredly, this will be the place where you will find yourself as an inspiration to others.

Benefits of Speakers

We believe that every interaction we have is an opportunity to make positive impact on others. We proffer young leaders as speakers to share their experiences and skills among students. The main purpose is to stimulate the young minds through inspiring talks. For speakers who believe in thought sharing process, “Young ones” is an inevitable network.
